Bio

I hold a Ph.D. in Microbiology with a focus on soil and water science in agricultural systems. My research background drives how I think about building technology — grounded in real science, designed for real operations.

I'm building practical systems that connect research data, field context, and decision workflows for growers and agricultural operators. The goal: make soil and agronomic intelligence usable in the field, not just in reports.
